The Apprentices project can be based on the following:
● A Specific problem
● A Recurring Issue 
● An Idea or Opportunity 
The Apprentice may work as part of a team to complete the project and must 
submit a project report with a 6,000-word count. 
In the Presentation with Questions the apprentice delivers a presentation to an 
independent assessor on their project. The presentation and questions must last 
typically 60 Minutes.

Assessment Methods: Project with a report Presentation with Questions and answers
