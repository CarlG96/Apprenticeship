# Business Rules

Business Rules are the things the business must abide by: eg laws of physics, legal, things that need to be
in place for the business to work properly

## Rules

- Business rules should be written in natural language
- Business rules should use consistent terminology
- Business rules should not have duplicates

## Operative Rules

Operative rules state what must happen in particular circumstances, eg

`Each flight booking request for a return journey must specify the return date`

Operative rules can be violated, but they simply describe the ideal behaviour.

## Definitional Rules

Describe the physical world and concepts, cannot be violated under any circumstances:

`Each flight must happen in the air`
