# Research Network Equipment From Packet Tracer

Cisco 2911

Cisco 2911 is part of the Cisco 2900 Series Integrated Services Routers. This means it looks it used to network with things outside the LAN (Local Area Network), Cisco 2911 also has LAN connectivity options

Cisco 3560

Cisco 3560 series is a line of fixed-configuration, enterprise class-switches. These switches are designed for small enterprise LAN offices or branch office environments. Has advanced layer 2 (LAN base) and layer 3 (IP base) abilities.

Cat6A Ethernet Cable

Layer 1, advanced version of the cat6 ethernet cable. Supports data transfer speeds of up to 10Gbps over a distance of 100 meters.
