In this module, you will further develop your knowledge and 
skills of software development by learning about Object 
Orientated Programming; that is how to design, develop and 
test these types of applications as well as learn associated 
good practice. Your knowledge, skills and behaviours will be 
evidenced through the development of a server-based 
application.

Credits: 20 

Assessment Method: 20-Minute Demonstration of implementing and testing an object orientated server solution
