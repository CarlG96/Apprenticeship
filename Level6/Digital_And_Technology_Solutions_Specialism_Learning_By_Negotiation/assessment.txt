A reflective learning e-portfolio in an agreed field of study will be 
identified, agreed by the apprentice, employer and TDM Coaches. An 
employer-responsive Individual Learning Plan (ILP Contract) will be 
developed. This ILP will identify the relevant negotiated learning 
outcomes, the learning process for their achievement, the assessment 
strategy to be undertaken and the mapping of the work to the 
apprenticeship outcomes (KSBs). Where appropriate the portfolio may 
link to an agreed professional qualification at an appropriate level.

Credits: 20

Assessment Method: A hyperpersonalized, employerresponsive eportfolio
